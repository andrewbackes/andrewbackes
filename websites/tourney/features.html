What can you do with Tourney?


Tourney is simple and lightweight. You can easily:

Play Tournaments

With Tourney, you can play tournaments between chess engines. Tourney requires very little overhead. It will ensure that legal games are played with precise time control (down to the millisecond).

Watch over the Web

Tourney acts as a web server. Anybody can watch a tournament from anywhere. Complete games can be played back along with detailed analysis.

Use Multiple Computers

You can host a tournament on one computer and then play it from other computers. This can be done over the web or locally. The connecting computer doesn't even need to have any engines installed.

Features:

  • Support for most UCI and Winboard engines.
  • Simple opening book (build from a pgn).
  • Time controls specified down to the millisecond.
  • Results outputted in PGN and plain text (customizable and useful for tuning).
  • Legality verification for engine moves.
  • Command line arguments for automation.
