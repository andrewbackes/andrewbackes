How does it work?

At this point, Dirty-Bit is strictly a chess engine. That means it is a console application that accepts commands and responds to those commands alone. This isnt exactly convenient for those people who just want to play some chess. Luckily, there are many 3rd party chess GUIs that act as a catalyst between the human player and the chess engine. While a custom GUI is in the scope of the Dirty-Bit project, it has not yet been rolled out. Below you can find the engine executables and links to popular GUIs.
